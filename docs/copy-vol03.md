## What changed in this revision (Uri's 19/7 comments)
1.  **Mesh and micron split out on the bond pages.** Metal Bond, Resin Bond, and CBN now carry explicit Mesh and Micron sections, each with its own applications and its own spec table, followed by a short Coated section (and PCBN on the CBN page). This mirrors the Natural Grit & Powder page. Mesh and micron tables are kept separate, since the two sizing systems do not correlate.
2.  **Tone pulled toward the technical buyer.** Several teaching-style passages were tightened into process language a qualified engineer reads faster (hero blocks, the "how it works" sections, the automotive outcome block, and the friability explanation). Roughly eight places in total.
3.  **Repetition reduced.** "Batch-to-batch consistency" and "QC-tested" are varied through the doc: lot-to-lot repeatability, consistent order to order, graded and inspected in-house, verified in our laboratory, the same grade every reorder.
4.  **Heroes tightened.** CVD Single Crystal and a few other hero blocks were shortened, with detail moved down the page.
5.  **Headings de-SEO'd.** Every "Where X is used" heading is now "Typical Applications."
6.  **"QC-upgraded" removed.** Replaced with plain production language (processed and graded to your specification), since it read as marketing and repeated inside its own paragraph.
7.  **Crystal strength claims softened.** We no longer imply crystal strength is tested on every batch. Quality copy now reads: particle size distribution and morphology on every lot, with crystal strength and coating coverage where required. Shape factor is attributed to mesh only.
8.  **Dental hub reordered.** Natural diamond grit (mesh) now leads the dental product list, as the single most-used dental material, ahead of metal bond and micron powder.
9.  **Hero eyebrow updated** per Uri's suggested line.
10. **Products Overview (Page 2) marked as removed.** Per the nav decision, clicking "Products" scrolls to the homepage product section and the mega-menu exposes the eight pages. The one-line descriptors are retained here only as the source for the mega-menu and homepage grid.
Open items still needing Uri: every [confirm with Uri] spec figure; the ISO 9001 certificate scan, number, and validity; the MCD and PCBN 301s; current SDS/MSDS files (EU uses SDS under REACH/CLP); and whether the eyebrow keeps "& CBN" (see Page 1 note).

**Coated metal bond diamond**
Coating a particle before it goes into the bond gives the matrix more to hold. 1.  **Incoming inspection.** Raw materials are tested on arrival against their incoming specification before anything enters production.
2.  **In-process control.** Production parameters are monitored and recorded through grading, coating, and finishing.
3.  **Final QC.** Every finished batch is sampled and tested in our laboratory, and results are compared against your specification and our internal standards.
4.  **Certificate of analysis.** Issued with any shipment on request, documenting the results for that specific lot.
5.  **Retention samples.** A sample from every batch is kept, so any later question can be checked against the exact material that shipped.
